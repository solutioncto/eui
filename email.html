Thank you for using our calculator and requesting this report. We hope it helps you gain insight into how your home energy use compares to others.

The report shows how your home consumption per square foot compares to the average home in a Department of Energy dataset for your climate zone. If you are:

  1. BUYING A HOUSE? -- USE THIS CALCULATOR!! -- If you are in the market to buy, use this to gain valuable insight on homes you are looking at! If the home has higher than average energy use, it may have serious indoor health, comfort, control, and/or durability problems!
  2. SELLING: If you are selling and your home shows lower than average consumption, you might want to leave the report on your kitchen counter for potential buyers to see. If your consumption is higher than average you might want to simply leave your bills on the counter, and NOT the report. Interestingly, studies show that just the act of sharing bills can lead to faster sales. And without a comparable tool like this, buyers probably won’t be put off by your bills unless they are REALLY crazy high.
  3. FIXING PROBLEMS: Many find our calculator because they are trying to solve comfort, health, or moisture problems in their homes.
